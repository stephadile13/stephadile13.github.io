About Us



TannerHecht Architecture is a full-service design firm dedicated to creating projects that enrich their communities. Since 1980, founders Jim Tanner and David Hecht have served a broad array of public and private-sector clients, bringing a hands-on, highly collaborative design approach to each project. TannerHecht’s work in urban infill developments, multi-stakeholder community- based projects, and commercial interiors has been recognized by awards and publications, regionally and nationally.

The firm is based in California with operations in both San Francisco and San Diego.
